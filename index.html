PruSelect Vantage Premier

A Single Premium Investment-Linked Policy

Your Gateway to Investment

Plan Type

A single premium policy that allows you to invest in a selection of funds available for this plan.

Minimum Investment

S$50,000

Minimum single premium to start.

Currency & Scheme

Available in Singapore Dollars (SGD) for both cash and Supplementary Retirement Scheme (SRS) plans.

How Your Investment Starts

Premium Allocation

A significant portion of your single premium or top-up amount is used to purchase units in the PruSelect Fund(s) you have selected. You can choose to invest in one or multiple funds. A minimum of 5% must be invested in any chosen fund.

97.5%

of your single premium or top-up is used to buy units.

Key Benefits & Bonuses

Death Benefit

We pay a benefit upon the death of the last surviving life assured.


Accidental Death

We pay the higher of the value of all units in your Account, or 105% of the single premium paid plus any top-ups (less any withdrawals). This coverage is up to the Policy Anniversary before the last surviving life assured turns 85.


Non-Accidental Death

We pay the value of all units in your Account.

Bonuses

Your investment is rewarded for its tenure and value.


Second-Year Bonus

If your single premium or top-up is at least S$250,000, you receive a bonus at the start of the second year.
• 0.50% for S$250,000 - S$499,999
• 0.75% for S$500,000 and above


Loyalty Bonus

A bonus of 0.5% of the Average Account Value is paid after every 60-month period for each tranche, provided requirements are met.

Understanding the Charges

Administration & Investment Charges


Administration Charge

An administration charge of 0.8% p.a. of the latest policy account value is billed monthly from the second month. This charge is funded through the cancellation of units.


Fund Management Charge

This is deducted by the respective Investment Manager of each PruSelect Fund. The unit prices are net of this charge.

Withdrawal / Surrender Charge

A charge applies if you make a partial withdrawal or surrender your policy within the first 24 months of the premium or top-up date.

No. of Completed Months* Charge Rate
1 - 12 months (Year 1) 1.6%
13 - 24 months (Year 2) 0.9%
25 months onwards (Year 3+) 0%

Flexibility for Your Financial Journey

  • Top-up Premium: Add funds with a minimum of S$20,000.
  • Fund Switching: Switch units to other available PruSelect Funds.
  • Partial Withdrawals: Withdraw a minimum of S$1,000 from your account.
  • Surrender Policy: You may apply to surrender your policy at any time.
  • Change Life Assured: Add or delete up to 3 lives assured (max twice).

Important Considerations

Buying a life insurance policy is a long-term commitment. Early termination usually involves high costs and the surrender value may be less than the total premiums paid. Investments are subject to investment risks, including the possible loss of the principal amount invested. The value of the units may fall as well as rise.
